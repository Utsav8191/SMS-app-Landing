# School Management System (SMS)

# Landing Website Design System & UI Specification (V1)

## Purpose

Design a premium, modern, trustworthy, and conversion-focused website for a School Management System SaaS platform.

The website should appeal to:

* School Owners
* Principals
* School Administrators
* Academic Coordinators
* Educational Trusts
* Educational Institutions

The visual experience should communicate:

* Trust
* Professionalism
* Simplicity
* Scalability
* Digital Transformation

Avoid looking like a traditional ERP system.

The product should feel like a modern SaaS platform comparable to industry-leading B2B software products.

---

# Design Principles

## 1. Premium First

Every section should feel clean, spacious, and premium.

Use:

* Large white spaces
* Rounded corners
* Soft shadows
* Clean typography
* Consistent spacing

Avoid:

* Dense layouts
* Excessive borders
* Overly colorful sections
* Outdated ERP-style interfaces

---

## 2. Education + Technology

Blend educational warmth with enterprise reliability.

Visual direction:

* Students
* Teachers
* Administrators
* Digital dashboards
* School operations

without appearing childish.

---

## 3. Conversion Driven

Every page should encourage one primary action:

### Book a Demo

Primary CTA throughout the website.
